Read-me!

Bonus chapters are contained side stories that can be read in isolation.

Pages before the bonus chapter Two Became Three are read from right-to-left.

I began drawing the comic for AnK back in 2007. This was before I had a solid foundation in world building or even a fleshed-out plotline. I was also a teenager with questionable writing skills. Chapters 1-13 are subpar in terms of execution and outdated in terms of content. I did not start drawing the comic digitally until over halfway through chapter 18, and my scans for prior pages were not as high resolution as they should have been. I have posted everything here for the sake of continuity, but I urge serious readers to look at the scripts for the early chapters (1-13), if not for all chapters. My scripts are all up-to-date and quick reads. I will eventually redraw arc 1, but not until I have finished the first draft in its entirety, by which point I expect to be proficient in all the areas of art I still need to develop. My next draft will be the final one, so I want it to be good.
